My Skills

Site Mapping

This is one of my favorite parts of UX/UI. By looking at the navigation in a sitemap, it makes it easier to see redundant links. This is also helpful to create different sitemaps and do user testing to see which navigation system is most intuitive.

Wirefames

While I enjoy all types of wireframing, digital mockups at the low fidelity level is my preferred stage. Creating a general design and playing around with different layouts to see which one works best not only improves user experience, but also helps to save time in later stages of design.

Usability Testing

With usability testing, I prefer to be the facilitator, guiding the person through the interview. Once the data has been collected, I use it to update the sitemap and wireframes to reflect the outcomes.

My Work

Roadtrip Rations Travel App

People are going over budget due to the costs of eating out, especially while traveling. Roadtrip Rations believes that planning meals to make yourself and having money set aside dedicated to eating out will help our users. By adding a meal planning section and section for eating out expenses. We will ease travelers worries of going over budget by meal planning and setting money aside for eating out.

NSPKU Website Redesign

Our team redesigned the NSPKU website leading with empathy for those impacted by this disease and striving to improve overall site experience for all users. We embarked on this project ensuring to keep those impacted by PKU at the forefront of all redesigned features with the understanding that it can be a challenging new world for individuals looking to NSPKU.org for resources.
